Jack Milano. A template restaurant website with a cms (and an inviting menu).

The
menu

What do you want to eat? The menu il directly taken from a Google Sheet, converted to Json and then parsed.
In this way, if the owner wants to change, add or remove something from it, he can simply edit the sheet (sounds difficult right?).

The
reservation

Hungry after seeing the menu? Let's book a table! The reservation page form sends data directly to she sheet. In this way, the owner can easily collect all the reservations.
The Google Sheet works exactly as a CMS.

Works on
any device

According to this, 49.06% of the users use a smartphone to visit websites. This website is fully responsive and mobile friendly.
